Common questions

Where is my data?
Your vehicles, records and receipt images are stored locally on your iPhone. Sijill 1.0 does not provide cloud sync.

How do I back up or share a history?
Open a vehicle and choose Export History to create a PDF. Save or share it using the iOS share sheet.

How do reminders work?
Dates and odometer targets appear inside Sijill. Optional device notifications can be enabled in Settings and are scheduled locally.

What is included free?
The free plan includes 1 vehicle, 5 service records, 1 receipt photo and 1 completed PDF export. Existing data remains viewable and editable after a limit is reached. Sijill Pro removes all four limits with a one-time purchase.

How do I restore Sijill Pro?
Open Settings or the Pro screen and choose Restore Purchases while signed in to the Apple Account used for the purchase.

How do I delete everything?
Use Delete All Data in Sijill Settings, or delete the app from your iPhone.

Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026

Our approach

Sijill is designed to keep your vehicle records on your device. Compact Refined Apps does not collect, transmit, sell, rent or use your vehicle profiles, maintenance records, costs, notes, reminders or receipt images.

Data stored on your iPhone

The app stores information you enter and receipt images you select in its local app container. You can delete all app data in Settings. Deleting the app also removes its local data, subject to how iOS device backups are managed by you and Apple.

Photos, notifications and exports

  • Sijill receives only the receipt image you choose through Apple’s system Photos picker.
  • Optional reminders are scheduled locally after you grant notification permission.
  • PDF histories leave the app only when you choose a destination in the system share sheet. The destination’s privacy terms then apply.

Purchases

Sijill Pro purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your payment-card details. Apple may process purchase and account information under Apple’s own privacy policy.

No tracking or advertising

Sijill contains no advertising SDK, third-party analytics SDK or cross-app tracking. Version 1.0 has no developer-operated account or cloud service.

Support and this website

If you email support, we receive the email address and information you choose to send so we can reply. This website is hosted by GitHub Pages, whose infrastructure may process standard web requests under GitHub’s privacy terms.

Privacy, in plain language

ChangePlot: Wardrobe Privacy Policy · Effective July 16, 2026

Information stored on your device

Productions, performers, roles, looks, costume pieces, show order, timing estimates, change plans, dresser steps, locations, notes, settings, and temporary checklist state are stored in the app’s local storage. ChangePlot does not transmit this information to the developer.

No account, analytics, advertising, or tracking

ChangePlot has no account system, developer server, cloud sync, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, tracking technology, or third-party SDK. The app does not collect data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

Exporting and sharing

Information leaves the app only when you choose to export, back up, or share it and select a destination. The selected destination handles that information under its own privacy terms. Review the content, recipient, and destination before sharing. Avoid unnecessary confidential or sensitive production information.

Retention and deletion

Local information remains until you delete it in the app, restore or replace local data through a chosen backup flow, or remove the app. Removing ChangePlot deletes its locally stored information from that device.

Support email

If you email support, your message, email address, and any information you choose to include are transmitted to and processed by the email providers used by you and the developer. They are used to answer the request, troubleshoot the app, and meet applicable legal obligations. Do not include confidential production material or sensitive personal information. You may request deletion of a support message, subject to legal retention requirements.

Children

ChangePlot is a general-audience production-planning utility and is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from any user.

Changes and contact

If these practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before the change takes effect. For privacy questions, email compactrefinedapps@outlook.com.

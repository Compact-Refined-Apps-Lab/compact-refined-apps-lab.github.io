Privacy, in plain language

MemoFuse Privacy Policy · Effective July 18, 2026

Audio stays on your device

Selected audio, private working copies, clip names, order, trim ranges, fades, previews, and merged results are processed and stored locally by MemoFuse. The app does not transmit this information to the developer.

No account, analytics, advertising, or tracking

MemoFuse has no account system, developer server, cloud sync, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, tracking technology, or third-party SDK. The app does not collect data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

No recording or microphone access

MemoFuse edits existing files selected by you. This version does not record audio and does not request microphone, camera, contacts, photo library, or location access.

Importing and sharing

The system document picker or another app provides only files you choose. An exported result leaves MemoFuse only when you select a destination in Apple's share sheet. File providers and chosen destinations handle data under their own privacy terms. Review the content, recipient, and destination before sharing.

Retention and deletion

Local working files and settings remain until you remove them within MemoFuse or delete the app. Removing MemoFuse deletes its locally stored information from that device. Files you previously saved or shared outside the app remain with their destination.

Support email

If you email support, your message, email address, and any information you choose to include are processed by the email providers used by you and the developer. They are used to answer the request, troubleshoot MemoFuse, and meet applicable legal obligations. Do not attach private audio or unnecessary sensitive information. You may request deletion of a support message, subject to legal retention requirements.

Children

MemoFuse is a general-audience utility and is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from any user.

Changes and contact

If these practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before the change takes effect. For privacy questions, email compactrefinedapps@outlook.com.

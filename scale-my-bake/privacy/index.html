Privacy, in plain language

Scale My Bake Privacy Policy · Effective July 16, 2026

Information stored on your device

Recipe and pan inputs, baker’s-percentage values, unit-conversion inputs, settings, and up to 20 saved calculation summaries are stored in the app’s local storage. Scale My Bake does not transmit this information to the developer.

No account, analytics, advertising, or tracking

Scale My Bake has no account system, developer server, cloud sync, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, tracking technology, or third-party SDK. The app does not collect data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

Sharing

A calculation leaves the app only when you choose Share and select a destination in Apple’s share sheet. The selected destination handles that information under its own privacy terms. Review the content and destination before sharing.

Retention and deletion

Local information remains until you delete an entry, clear History, or remove the app. Removing Scale My Bake deletes its locally stored information from that device.

Support email

If you email support, your message, email address, and any information you choose to include are transmitted to and processed by the email providers used by you and the developer. Support messages are used to answer the request, troubleshoot the app, and meet applicable legal obligations. Do not include sensitive or unnecessary personal information. You may request deletion of a support message, subject to legal retention requirements.

Children

Scale My Bake is a general-audience kitchen utility and is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from any user.

Changes and contact

If these practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before the change takes effect. For privacy questions, email compactrefinedapps@outlook.com.

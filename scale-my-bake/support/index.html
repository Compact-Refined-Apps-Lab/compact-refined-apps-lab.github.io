How can we help?

Scale My Bake is a focused offline toolkit for common baking and kitchen calculations. No account, subscription, or network connection is required.

Recipe scaling

Fractions and serving sizes

Enter the original and target servings, then add ingredient amounts. Scale My Bake supports decimals, simple fractions, mixed fractions, and common fraction characters.

Pans and units

Compare pans or convert a kitchen unit

Use Pans for round, square, and rectangular pan estimates. Use Convert for supported temperature, weight, and volume units. Weight-to-volume conversion is not offered because ingredient density varies.

History

Where are saved calculations stored?

History is stored only on your device and holds up to 20 calculation summaries. You can delete individual entries or clear the full history in the app. Removing the app also removes its local data.

Accuracy

Are results guaranteed?

No. Ingredient density, measuring method, pan construction, oven calibration, altitude, fill level, and technique can change real outcomes. Independently verify important calculations and follow reliable recipes, labels, manufacturer instructions, doneness methods, and food-safety guidance.

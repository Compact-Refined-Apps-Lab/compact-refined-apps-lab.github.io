How can we help?

KinVitals helps households organize blood-pressure and glucose readings. It does not measure, interpret, diagnose, classify readings, recommend treatment, or replace professional care.

Record

Add readings and useful context

Create a family profile, then add a blood-pressure session or glucose reading from Today. KinVitals stores the values and context you enter. Input limits catch likely typing, unit, or measurement-protocol mistakes; they are not medical judgments.

Trends and reports

Prepare for handovers and appointments

Review saved values in Trends. Create a PDF from Care when you want to share an organized record with a clinician or family member. Always check the report and recipient before sharing.

Compatible meters

Bluetooth import is standards-based

KinVitals Pro can scan for nearby devices that expose the standard Bluetooth SIG Blood Pressure or Glucose service. Proprietary vendor protocols and direct CGM connections are not supported. Review every imported value before saving it.

Apple Health

Sync only the profile marked as Me

KinVitals Pro can exchange supported blood-pressure and glucose samples with Apple Health after you grant permission. Health access is optional and controlled in iOS Settings. KinVitals does not use Health data for advertising or tracking.

Pro and purchases

Annual or lifetime access

Pro unlocks additional profiles, longer trends, family handover, flexible reports, more reminders, Apple Health, compatible Bluetooth import, backup, restore, and CSV export. The App Store shows your current localized price and offer terms. Use Restore Purchases in KinVitals if a completed purchase is not recognized.

Your data

Local by default

KinVitals has no account or developer cloud. Records stay on your device and are excluded from ordinary device cloud backup. PDF, CSV, and backup files leave KinVitals only when you choose a destination. Deleting the app removes its local records; files you previously exported remain with their destination.

Safety

Get appropriate help

If you are concerned about a reading, symptoms, medication, or treatment, contact a qualified healthcare professional. For urgent or emergency concerns, use the emergency services available in your location.

Contact

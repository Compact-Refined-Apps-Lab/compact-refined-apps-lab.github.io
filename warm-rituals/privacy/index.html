Privacy, in plain language

Warm Rituals Privacy Policy · Effective July 12, 2026

What stays on your device

Your onboarding preferences, ritual completions, energy check-ins, favorites, reminder settings, path progress, and limited Premium funnel events are stored in the app’s local storage. Warm Rituals does not transmit this information to us.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Warm Rituals receives only the StoreKit information needed to determine whether Premium access is active. We do not receive your payment card details. Apple’s handling of purchase data is governed by Apple’s privacy policy.

Notifications

If you choose to enable reminders, notification schedules are created on your device. You can change or disable them at any time in Warm Rituals or in iOS Settings.

Analytics and tracking

Warm Rituals currently uses no advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, or cross-app identifiers. Premium funnel events remain on your device and are capped at 100 entries.

Retention and deletion

Local information remains until you change it, remove the app, or erase the app’s data. Deleting Warm Rituals removes its locally stored wellness and analytics information from that device. App Store purchase history is managed by Apple.

Children and health information

Warm Rituals provides general wellbeing education and does not diagnose or treat health conditions. It is not designed to collect medical records or information from HealthKit.

Contact and changes

For privacy questions, email compactrefinedapps@outlook.com. If these practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before the change takes effect.

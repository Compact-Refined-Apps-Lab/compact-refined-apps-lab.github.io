How can we help?

Warm Rituals is designed to be calm and simple. These answers cover the most common questions about reminders, saved progress, and Premium access.

Premium

Restore an existing subscription

Open Warm Rituals, choose You, open Warm Rituals Premium, then tap Restore purchases. Make sure the device is signed in to the Apple Account used for the original purchase.

Reminders

A reminder did not appear

Check your chosen times under You → Ritual reminders, then confirm notifications are allowed in iOS Settings → Notifications → Warm Rituals. All reminder schedules are stored on your device.

Your data

Where is my ritual history stored?

Your preferences, energy check-ins, favorites, completions, and path progress stay in local app storage. Removing the app also removes this local history. Warm Rituals does not currently offer account sync.

Wellbeing

Is Warm Rituals medical advice?

No. Warm Rituals provides general wellbeing education and cultural context. It does not diagnose, prevent, or treat medical conditions. Stop any practice that causes pain or unusual symptoms and seek appropriate professional care.

Contact

Still need help?

Email compactrefinedapps@outlook.com and include your iOS version, Warm Rituals app version, and a short description of what happened. Please do not send medical records or other sensitive health information.
